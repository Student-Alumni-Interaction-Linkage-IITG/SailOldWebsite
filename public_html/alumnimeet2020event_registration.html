Event Registration!

  1. UMatter: The alumni who’ve excelled at their respective fields will be interacting with the next generation of young minds that will be coming out of the campus, the students, and will be talking about various topics. We have two sub-events under the same banner namely,
    i.Your Story: Alumni are invited to take the stage, and share their story with the IITG Junta. Be it memories of the first class that you bunked, or the first big obstacle in the corporate world- we are all ears!
    Durations: 15 mins per speaker
    Total number of registrations: 05
    ii. Panel Discussion: Be the part of a distinguished Alumni Panel, and share a free-flowing conversation with other panel members and interact with the students in the crowd.
    Duration: 30mins per panel
    Total number of Panel: 02
    NOTE: In case of higher participation, preference will be given to the older alumni.

  2. Startup Showcase: We have a talk by our alumni about their startup journey. We are planning to give 10 min to each alum but that time is tentative depending on the number of alumni that show up. Talks will be followed by QnA and Networking time. All Alumni will be invited for the event to attend along with campus students.
    Duration: 10mins per presentation
    Total number of Registrations: NA

Personality Development Program helps you make re-visits to your departments or hostels or classrooms where you can do one to one interaction with your juniors and train them to develop their personality with all the knowledge and experiences you have gathered so far.
