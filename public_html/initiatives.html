AMP

AMP is Live now!

Everybody needs a mentor. A person who can guide us and help us achieve our goals through his vast experience. And who better to do this, than someone who has been in our shoes during his time, our alumni. Keeping this in mind, we have initiated the Alumni Mentorship Program(AMP), a mentorship program in which alumni mentor the current students of the campus. This provides a platform for the alumni and the students to interact with each other, wherein the alumni get to know about the recent developments in the campus, and the students get to learn a lot from the alumni.

We, the Sail team, invite the alumni/alumnae of IITG to become mentors and students of IITG to become mentees click the buttons below to get yourself registerd.

Alumni Greet

As freshers, we are quite intrigued and excited about the idea of joining a college. Along with this, we also have a lot of doubts regarding the campus and the Institute in general. 'Alumni Greet' is an initiative by SAIL, in which, a self-shot welcome video, made by an alumnus /alumna from a hostel, is played at the hostel orientation, for the freshers.


Class Song

Every year, SAIL releases the class song, as a farewell gift to the graduating batch, in which all the students of the graduating batch come together to perform for a song, as a memory of the four incredible years that they have spent in the IITG campus.

SPARC

SPARC, abbreviated as Students' PAN IIT Alumni Relations Cell, is an umbrella organization formed with the conjunction of the Alumni Relations bodies of all the Indian Institutes of Technology. SPARC, as an organization, hosts various events that are not confined to a single IIT but involve all the IITs in the country. As a part of SPARC, the first ever PAN IIT level Student Alumni Meet was organized recently on an international stage at Singapore.


Year Book

College life is filled with incredible moments and we take some of them with us as memories. In the form of Yearbook, SAIL provides a way for the final year students to re-live some of those memorable moments in the words and pics of their friends. It is the memories that live on and the sweetest of them are made here at IITG!
